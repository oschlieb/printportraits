A bit about me

Whilst at school I started to train as graphic designer. I worked in a small local graphic design agency, in my own time. We creating print brochures and exhibition stands for a variety of clients such as the MoD and Vosper Thornycroft.

After leaving school I attended Art College in Portsmouth. At Portsmouth I spent six years developing my skills as a Fine Artist specialising in Conceptual Art.

I'm currently a Strategy & Marketing consultant for small businesses and art has always been part of my life. I still love the way that ideas influence art rather than

simply focusing on the aesthetics, which is why this project excites me so much.

There is a lot I want to try out with this idea and new ideas every day, hope that you can enjoy this too.
